DELTAVOICE Account Deletion Request

Users of DELTAVOICE can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Open the DELTAVOICE app and go to:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

Option 2: Email us at:

deltavoicekb@gmail.com

Verification: To protect accounts from unauthorized deletion, all deletion requests must be verified. If you request deletion via email, we will send a confirmation to the email address associated with your account. In-app requests require re-authentication. The 30-day deletion period begins after the request is verified; you may cancel within that window by contacting support.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Profile information
  • Voice processing history
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Files uploaded by the user

Data Retention

Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days of a verified deletion request.

Some information may be retained temporarily if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup recovery purposes. Such retained data is deleted automatically according to our retention policies.

Contact

For questions regarding account deletion, contact: deltavoicekb@gmail.com or review our Privacy Policy for more details.
